  • Press on “START GAME” button and the game will start. Each game lasts 10 spins.
  • On the left of the screen, press on the arrow at the stake and select the amount you’re betting on in this game.
  • This is the reel. Each spin the user will see numbers and symbols on the reel.
  • This is the grid. Each spin, numbers will be marked with the aim to create lines from 5 numbers.
  • Pressing the “START GAME” will spin the reel and give you numbers or symbols. Matching numbers on the reel will be marked on the grid.
  • When you spin the reel and you get the devil symbol, it means that you lost a chance to receive a number in that slot.
  • When you spin the reel and you get a free spin symbol, it means that you got another spin turn. A yellow ball will be added to the lot.
  • When you spin the reel and you get a joker symbol, it means that you can chose any number to mark from the row above the joker.
  • When you complete a line, it is marked on the bonus ladder on the left side. There are 12 option to complete a line.
  • When you spin the reel and you get a super joker symbol, it means that you can chose any number on the grid. We need to add this text and animation here.
  • When you complete 5 lines or more, you get to play the bonus games.
  • In order to play the bonus game you need to press the collect button.
    Remember: The amount of money in the stack, that was defined in the start, is the win multiplier in a bonus games
  • You can choose from 3 wishing wells to reveal a prize multiplier. This prize multiplier will be applied to the win multiplier, that was defined by user in the stack.
